Ciders can be classified from dry to sweet. Their appearance ranges from cloudy with sediment to completely clear, and their colour ranges from almost colourless to amber to brown. The variation and colour are mostly due to filtering between pressing and fermentation.

Trees

Merry Christmas

Evergreen trees have traditionally been used to celebrate winter festivals for thousands of years. Pagans used evergreen trees to decorate their homes during the winter , as it made them think of the spring to come. The Romans used Fir Trees to decorate their temples at the festival of Saturnalia.
